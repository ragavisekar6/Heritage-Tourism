Hawwa Masjid (NGO Colony, Dindigul)

An Oasis of Spirituality Amidst the Sands of Time!

The walls of this mosque have witnessed centuries of unwavering devotion.

Mosque Highlights

The Hawwa Masjid in NGO Colony is a well-known mosque in Dindigul, offering a peaceful space for worship and community engagement. Known for its modern Islamic architecture, the mosque is a place where Muslims gather for daily prayers, Quranic studies, and spiritual discussions.The mosque also plays an active role in social welfare, offering assistance to the needy, conducting educational programs, and promoting harmony. During Eid, the mosque hosts special sermons, mass prayers, and community feasts, bringing people together in celebration.
