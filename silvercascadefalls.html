Silver Cascade Falls (Kodaikanal)

Nature’s Curtain of Silver – A Timeless Spectacle!

Watch the waters shimmer like silk as they tumble from dizzying heights.

Falls Highlights

Silver Cascade Waterfalls is one of the first attractions tourists encounter on their way to Kodaikanal. This 180-foot waterfall is formed by the overflow of Kodaikanal Lake, creating a spectacular sight as the water plunges down the rocky cliffs. The surrounding greenery and the sound of gushing water make it a perfect spot for a quick stop and photography. The falls are especially stunning during the monsoon season when the water flow is at its peak. Visitors can also explore the nearby fruit and souvenir stalls selling local produce such as fresh carrots, plums, and handmade chocolates. While swimming is not allowed due to the force of the water, the area around the falls offers a refreshing break from the journey. If you're looking for a scenic roadside attraction, Silver Cascade Waterfalls is a must-see in Kodaikanal.

Opening Hours

  • 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Best Time to Visit

  • Monsoon Season (June to September): Visit during the monsoon for a spectacular view of the falls in full flow.
