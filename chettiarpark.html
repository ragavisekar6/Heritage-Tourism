Chettiar Park (Kodaikanal)

Under the Shade of Giants – Let the Trees Tell Their Tales!

Walk beneath ancient trees and feel the embrace of time itself.

Park Highlights

Chettiar Park is a hidden gem in Kodaikanal, offering a tranquil escape from the bustling tourist spots. This beautifully landscaped garden is known for its well-maintained lawns, exotic plant species, and vibrant seasonal flowers. The park is an ideal place for a relaxing stroll, a picnic, or simply enjoying nature's beauty. One of the main attractions of Chettiar Park is the famous Kurinji flower, which blooms once every twelve years, covering the area in a stunning purple hue. The park is home to a variety of rare trees and plants, making it a paradise for botany enthusiasts. The peaceful environment, combined with the chirping of birds and the fresh mountain air, creates a perfect setting for meditation and unwinding. Families, photographers, and couples often visit this park to enjoy its serene beauty. The best time to visit is during the monsoon and winter months when the flowers are in full bloom, and the weather is refreshingly cool. Whether you are looking for a quiet place to relax or a scenic spot for photography, Chettiar Park offers an unforgettable experience in Kodaikanal.
