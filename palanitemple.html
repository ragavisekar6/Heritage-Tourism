Arulmigu Dhandayudhapani Temple (Palani)

When the First Light of Dawn Touches the Temple Spire!

A sight that inspires, awakens, and connects the soul to the divine.

Temple Highlights

Perched atop Palani Hills, the Palani Murugan Temple is one of the most sacred and powerful temples dedicated to Lord Murugan. As one of the six abodes (Arupadai Veedu) of Lord Murugan, this temple holds immense religious significance for devotees.The legend of Palani Murugan Temple is fascinating—Lord Murugan, in a fit of divine disagreement with his parents, renounced his celestial abode and came to Palani as a young ascetic. The idol of Lord Murugan here is unique, made of a special Navapashanam (a sacred amalgamation of nine minerals) by the Siddhar saint, Bhogar.Pilgrims ascend the 693 steps leading to the temple as a mark of devotion, though ropeway and winch services are available for convenience. The Thai Poosam festival is the grandest celebration here, with lakhs of devotees performing Kavadi Attam (ritualistic dance with a decorated burden).The temple’s panoramic hilltop view, combined with its divine aura, makes it a must-visit. Devotees believe that visiting Palani and praying to Murugan removes obstacles in life, grants wisdom, and fulfills wishes. The famous Panchamirtham, a sacred prasadam made of bananas, jaggery, honey, and ghee, is a must-try here.

Opening Hours

  • 5:45 AM - 9:00 PM

Festive Seasons

  • Thaipusam: Celebrated on the full moon day of the Tamil month of Thai (January-February), it's marked by devotees carrying kavadis.
  • Panguni Uthiram: Occurs in the Tamil month of Panguni (March-April), featuring processions and special rituals.
  • Vaikasi Visakam: Celebrated in May-June, marking the birth anniversary of Lord Murugan.

Entry Fees:

Nearby Heritage Stays & Hotels

Famous Food Places

  • Hotel Subam: Known for authentic South Indian dishes, located around 600 meters from the temple.
  • New Tirupur Lodge: Offers a variety of traditional Tamil dishes, approximately 700 meters away.
  • Sri Patisserie: Renowned bakery offering sweets and savories, about 800 meters from the temple.
