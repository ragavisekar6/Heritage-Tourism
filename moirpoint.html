Moir Point (Kodaikanal)

Where Land Kisses the Sky – A View Worth a Thousand Words!

Witness the world’s most enchanting moments from nature’s best viewpoints.

Viewpoint Highlights

Moir Point serves as the gateway to Berijam Lake and offers one of the most breathtaking panoramic views in Kodaikanal. This scenic viewpoint is named after Sir Thomas Moir, who played a crucial role in constructing the Goschen Road in 1929. Perched at a high altitude, Moir Point provides visitors with stunning views of lush green valleys, mist-covered mountains, and the winding road leading to Berijam Lake. The refreshing mountain breeze and cool temperatures make it an ideal place for a quick stop during a sightseeing tour. A small watchtower at the viewpoint enhances the visitor experience by offering an even better perspective of the surrounding landscapes. Moir Point is also a great location for photography, as the ever-changing hues of the sky and mist-covered forests create a mesmerizing backdrop. Adventure seekers can explore nearby trekking trails that lead deeper into Kodaikanal's wilderness. The best time to visit is during the early morning or late afternoon when the mist adds a mystical touch to the scenery. Whether you are looking for a peaceful retreat or a picture-perfect moment, Moir Point is a must-visit destination in Kodaikanal.

  • Monsoon Season: Best time to visit for mist-covered views and lush green landscapes.
