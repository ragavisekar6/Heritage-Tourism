Seran's Fun park

A Silent Tribute to the Brave, Where Every Brick Holds a Story!

Step into the heart of remembrance, where courage and sacrifice are immortalized, and the spirit of the past whispers through the ages.

Park Highlights

Seran Fun Park is a popular recreational destination located in Theni district, Tamil Nadu. It's a family-friendly amusement spot known for its relaxed atmosphere, water-based activities, and scenic setting surrounded by the natural beauty of the Western Ghats.

Opening Hours

  • 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM.

Best Time to Visit

  • October to March (pleasant weather)

Entry Fees:

  • Adults: ₹100 – ₹150 (approx.)
  • Children: ₹50 – ₹100 (depending on age/height)

Transportation Options

  • TBy Road: Well-connected by bus or car from Theni town (about 10–15 km).
  • Nearest Railway Station: Theni Railway Station
  • Nearest Airport: Madurai International Airport (approx. 85 km)

Nearby Heritage Stays & Hotels

  • Hotel Western Gatz
  • Vairam Residency

Famous Food Places

  • On-site canteens serving snacks, refreshments, and local South Indian dishes.
  • Visitors can also bring picnic baskets.

Must-Try Specialties

  • Theni Mutton Biryani – Hotel Sri Gowri Shankar (Near Theni Bus Stand).
  • Seeraga Samba Biryani – Venu Biriyani Hotel (Periyakulam Road, Theni).
  • Ayira Meen Kuzhambu (Small Fish Curry) – Local Messes in Vaigai Dam area.
  • Banana Chips – Made from local Nendran bananas, available in street shops near Theni Market.
  • Karupatti Coffee – Traditional palm jaggery coffee from roadside tea stalls in Cumbum & Uthamapalayam.
  • Palani Panchamirtham – Available in shops near temples and local sweet shops across Theni.
  • Elaneer Payasam – Served in select restaurants near Lower Camp & Bodi Mettu.
