Masjid-e-Azam

Echoes of Faith in Every Dome and Arch!

Immerse yourself in the serene ambiance of these mosques, where the beauty of Islamic architecture meets the purity of prayer, creating a sacred space for all who visit.

Mosque Highlights

Masjid-e-Azam in Theni is known for its modern Islamic architecture with a spacious prayer hall accommodating over 2000 worshippers. The mosque features a beautiful central dome and four minarets, with excellent acoustics for Quran recitation. It serves as a center for Islamic learning with regular classes.

Opening Hours

  • 5:00 AM - 9:30 PM

Festive Seasons

  • Lailatul Qadr - Special night prayers during Ramadan
  • Islamic New Year - Religious lectures and programs
