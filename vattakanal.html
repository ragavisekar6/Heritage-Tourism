Vattakanal Falls (Kodaikanal)

A Journey Through the Mist – Let the Falls Wash Away Your Worries!

Step into a world where rushing waters cleanse the soul.

Falls Highlights

Vattakanal Falls is a mesmerizing waterfall tucked away in the lush forests of Kodaikanal. Known as the 'Little Israel of India' due to the influx of Israeli tourists, Vattakanal is a hidden paradise for nature lovers. The waterfall cascades down multiple rocky layers, creating a soothing sound that blends harmoniously with the chirping of birds and rustling leaves. The journey to the falls requires a short trek through dense greenery, making it an exciting adventure for trekkers. During the monsoon, the falls are in full glory, surrounded by mist, adding a mystical charm. The fresh mountain air and tranquil environment make this spot ideal for meditation and relaxation. With minimal commercialization, Vattakanal Falls remains a pristine natural wonder, offering an escape from bustling city life.

Opening Hours

  • Open 24 hours. It is better to visit during daytime.

Best Time to Visit

  • Monsoon Season (June–September): Best time to visit for a beautiful waterfall experience.

Entry Fees:

  • No such Entry fee.

Transportation Options

  • From Kodaikanal Bus Stand: 7 km.
  • Mode of Transport: Taxi, or Auto.
  • Fare: ₹200-₹250 for taxi.

Nearby Heritage Stays & Hotels
