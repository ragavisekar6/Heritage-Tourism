Pillar Rocks (Kodaikanal)

The Edge of Everything – Feel Infinite at Nature’s Best Kept Secret!

Stand tall at the cliffs of wonder and let your spirit soar.

Viewpoint Highlights

Pillar Rocks is one of the most iconic attractions in Kodaikanal, known for its stunning three vertical rock formations that stand 400 feet tall. These majestic pillars, surrounded by thick mist and lush greenery, create a breathtaking view that leaves visitors in awe. The viewpoint offers a spectacular sight of the deep valleys and dense forests stretching beyond the rocks. The small garden at the base adds to the charm of the place, providing a perfect spot for photography and relaxation. Legends say that these rocks hold a tragic love story, adding a touch of mystery to their beauty. The best time to visit is during the monsoon and winter seasons when the mist partially covers the pillars, creating an ethereal atmosphere. If you’re looking for a scenic and dramatic spot in Kodaikanal, Pillar Rocks should definitely be on your itinerary.

Opening Hours

  • 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Best time to visit:

  • Kodaikanal Summer Festival (May): The best time to visit Pillar Rocks for stunning panoramic views and seasonal beauty.
