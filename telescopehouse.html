Telescope House (Kodaikanal)

A Balcony to the Universe – The Best Seats for Nature’s Grand Show!

Sit back and soak in the drama of the changing skies.

Viewpoint Highlights

Perched at the northern tip of the famed Coaker's Walk in Kodaikanal, the Telescope House offers visitors an unparalleled opportunity to gaze upon the mesmerizing landscapes that define this hill station. This modest yet significant structure serves as a gateway to panoramic vistas, allowing one to behold the sprawling valleys, distant villages, and shimmering water bodies that dot the region.From this vantage point, the Sothupparai Dam, Vaigai Dam, Periyakulam town, and the meandering Varaha River come into clear view, each narrating its own story of nature's grandeur and human endeavor. The Telescope House transforms these distant sights into intimate experiences, bridging the gap between observer and landscape.Beyond its visual offerings, the Telescope House stands as a testament to Kodaikanal's commitment to enhancing the tourist experience, seamlessly blending natural beauty with thoughtful infrastructure. For those seeking a moment of awe and inspiration, a visit to the Telescope House is an essential chapter in the Kodaikanal journey.

Opening Hours

  • 7:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Best Time to Visit

  • Summer Festival: Great time for stargazing and learning about astronomy.
