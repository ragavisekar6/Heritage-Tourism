Dhyanalinga Temple (Coimbatore)

A Stillness So Profound, Even Time Stands Silent!

Beyond the threshold of thought lies a space where silence blooms, and every breath becomes a doorway to the divine.

Temple Highlights

Dhyanalinga is a unique meditative space and energy center established by Sadhguru at the Isha Yoga Center. It contains a mercury-based Shiva lingam energized to aid inner balance. Unlike traditional temples, it requires no rituals or mantras. The sanctum radiates powerful energies and welcomes people of all faiths. Adjacent to it is the Linga Bhairavi shrine and the towering 112-foot Adiyogi statue.
