Holy Redeemer’s Church

Where Faith Rests in Every Brick and Hope Soars in Every Prayer!

Step inside these magnificent churches and let the peaceful silence speak to your soul. With towering spires, colorful stained glass, and centuries-old hymns, they are sanctuaries of grace and devotion.

Church Highlights

Holy Redeemer’s Church in Andipatti is a serene place of worship offering spiritual retreats for visitors and devotees in the Andipatti region. Its peaceful surroundings make it an ideal destination for reflection.

Opening Hours

Festive Seasons

  • Feast of Holy Redeemer (August) – Processions & special prayers
