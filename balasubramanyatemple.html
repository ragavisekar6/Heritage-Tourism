Balasubramanya Temple

Myth, Majesty & Miracles – A Temple’s Timeless Embrace!

Every stone whispers legends of gods, saints, and devotees who shaped the soul of this divine space.

Temple Highlights

Balasubramanya Temple, located in Periyakulam, Theni district, is a revered ancient temple dedicated to Lord Murugan. Known for its beautiful Dravidian architecture and serene surroundings, the temple sits on the banks of the Varaha River. The temple is considered highly powerful for devotees seeking blessings for good health, prosperity, and success in endeavors. The annual Skanda Sashti festival and Panguni Uthiram celebrations draw thousands of devotees.

Opening Hours

6:00 AM – 12:00 PM, 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Festive Seasons

  • Skanda Sashti Festival (October/November) – Grand rituals and processions
  • Panguni Uthiram (March/April) – Special abhishekam and cultural programs
  • Karthigai Deepam – Temple illuminated beautifully with oil lamps

Entry Fees

  • Free

Transportation Options

  • Periyakulam Bus Stand (1 km away) – Auto fare approx. ₹30-₹50
  • Nearest Railway Station: Madurai Junction (80 km away) – Taxis and buses easily available
  • Local Buses:
    1. Madurai → Theni → Periyakulam (frequent bus services)

Nearby Heritage Stays & Hotels

  • Hotel Vairamaligai, Periyakulam – Clean and budget-friendly accommodation
  • Western Gatz Hotel, Theni – Premium hotel with excellent amenities (15 km away)

Famous Food Places

Must-Try Specialties

  • Paal Kozhukattai (Sweet Rice Dumplings in milk)
  • Traditional filter coffee at Periyakulam bus stand area
