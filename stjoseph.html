St. Joseph's Church

Under These Majestic Arches, Faith Finds Its Voice!

Every whispered prayer ascends to the heavens from these sacred walls.

Church Highlights

A beacon of faith in the region, St. Joseph’s Church is known for its sacred history and deep-rooted Christian values. The church has been a center of worship for decades, serving as a spiritual refuge for thousands.The beautiful interiors, calm atmosphere, and devotional services create an uplifting experience for visitors. The church is particularly famous for its choir and music services, which add a divine touch to every prayer session.During Christmas, Easter, and the Feast of St. Joseph, the church hosts grand celebrations with special prayers, charity events, and community feasts. Whether you are a pilgrim or a casual visitor, the church welcomes everyone seeking peace and divine blessings.

Opening Hours

  • 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM.Sunday services are usually held in the morning.

Festive Seasons

  • Feast of St. Joseph (March 19) – Major celebration for the patron saint.
  • Christmas, Good Friday & Easter – Observed with prayers and processions.
