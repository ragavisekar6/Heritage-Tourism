Begampur Big Mosque (Dindigul)

Where Every Bow in Prayer Draws You Closer to the Divine!

In this house of Allah, all worries dissolve in the embrace of faith.

Mosque Highlights

The Begampur Big Mosque is one of the oldest and most significant mosques in Dindigul, showcasing the grandeur of Islamic architecture. This mosque is a center of daily prayers, religious gatherings, and Islamic teachings.Built with intricate domes, towering minarets, and beautiful Islamic calligraphy, the mosque stands as a testament to centuries-old craftsmanship. The Friday prayers witness a large congregation, reflecting the deep-rooted faith of the Muslim community.During Ramadan and Eid, the mosque becomes a hub of spiritual activity, with special prayers, feasts, and charity drives for the underprivileged. The mosque serves as a symbol of unity, faith, and peace in the region.

Opening Hours

  • 6:00 AM to 8:30 PM, daily.

Festive Seasons

  • Ramadan – Special prayers, fasting, and grand Iftar gatherings.
  • Eid-ul-Fitr – Marked with morning prayers and feasts.
  • Eid-ul-Adha – Celebrated with prayers and food distribution.
  • Milad-un-Nabi – Observed with religious sermons and community events.

Transportation Options

  • Dindigul Bus Stand(1.5 km) - Auto fare: ₹30–₹50.
  • Dindigul Railway Station(2 km) - Auto fare: ₹40–₹60.

Entry Fees:

  • Free.
