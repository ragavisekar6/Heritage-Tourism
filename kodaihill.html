Kodaikanal (Princess of Hill Station)

Beyond the Hustle, Beneath the Stars – Hills That Heal!

Escape to a world where silence speaks louder than words.

Hill Highlights

Kodaikanal, often called the "Princess of Hill Stations," is a breathtaking retreat nestled in the Western Ghats of Tamil Nadu at an altitude of 2,133 meters (7,000 feet). Surrounded by mist-covered valleys, dense pine forests, and rolling meadows, Kodaikanal is famed for its serene lakes, scenic viewpoints, and rich biodiversity. The star-shaped Kodaikanal Lake is the town’s heart, offering boating and cycling experiences, while Coaker’s Walk and Dolphin’s Nose provide panoramic views of the vast plains below. The town’s cool climate, with temperatures rarely exceeding 20°C, makes it a perfect year-round getaway. Beyond its natural beauty, Kodaikanal is known for its homemade chocolates, organic eucalyptus oil, herbal teas, and fresh hill-grown vegetables. Adventure seekers can explore trekking trails like Perumal Peak and Vattakanal, while nature lovers can witness the rare Kurunji flower, which blooms once every 12 years, covering the hills in a stunning shade of blue. Whether you're seeking a romantic escape, a family vacation, or a solo retreat, Kodaikanal offers a unique blend of tranquility, adventure, and cultural charm, making it one of India's most enchanting hill stations.

Opening Hours

  • Open year-round, but it's best visited during the day for sightseeing and nature walks.

Best Time to Visit

  • Summer (March to May): Best weather, ideal for sightseeing and boating.
  • Monsoon (June to September): Lush greenery but slippery trails; misty views.
  • Winter (October to February): Cool, foggy, romantic vibes—perfect for cozy stays.

Entry Fees:

  • No such Entry fee.

Transportation Options

  • Public Transport: Regular TNSTC buses from Madurai, Palani, and Dindigul.
  • Nearest Railway Station: Kodai Road (80 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Madurai Airport (135 km)

Nearby Heritage Stays & Hotels

  • Budget Stays - JC Residency, Kodai Gate, Hotel Hilltop (₹800–₹2,000/night).
  • Mid-Range Hotels - The Fern Creek, Great Trails by GRT (₹2,500–₹5,000/night).
  • Luxury Resorts - Sterling Kodai Lake, The Carlton (₹6,000–₹12,000/night).
  • Heritage Stays - Villa Retreat, Kodai Heritage Resort (₹3,000–₹7,000/night).

Famous Food Places

  • Cloud Street Café – Wood-fired pizzas, pasta, pancakes.
  • Tava Vegetarian Restaurant – North Indian thalis, chaats.
  • Astoria Veg – Traditional South Indian meals.
  • Hilltop Inn (Near Lake) – Parottas, chicken fry, biryani.
  • Muncheez Restaurant – Wraps, burgers, milkshakes.

Must-Try Specialties

  • Homemade Chocolate – Danish Display, The Chocolate Factory, Cloud Street.
  • Cheese (Kodai Cheese) – Cheese Corner, Kodai Dairy Products.
  • Pastries & Fresh Bread – Pastry Corner, Daily Bread Pastry Shop.
  • Hot Chocolate – Pastry Corner, The Tredis Tea Room.
