Subramanya Temple (Pambar Shola, Kodaikanal)

From Every Devotee’s Heart, a Prayer Rises Like Incense to the Gods!

A sacred space where wishes take flight and blessings descend.

Temple Highlights

Tucked away in the lush embrace of Pambar Shola, the Subramanya Temple is one of Kodaikanal’s most soul-stirring spiritual hideaways. Dedicated to Lord Murugan (Subramanya), the god of courage and wisdom, this sacred spot blends untamed nature with the sacred silence of devotion, offering a surreal experience far removed from the everyday.As you trek through the dense forest paths of Pambar Shola—home to rare birds, butterflies, and whispering trees—you'll feel like you’re stepping into an ancient story. Just when the wild begins to feel endless, the temple emerges like a mystical oasis—humble, serene, and deeply powerful. Set against the backdrop of emerald green and birdsong, this small temple radiates peace that only nature-blessed shrines can offer.The temple is revered by locals and quietly visited by those who know its spiritual gravity. There's no crowd, no chaos—just the rustle of leaves, the soft chant of mantras, and the presence of divinity flowing through the mountain air. Pilgrims say prayers here are heard faster, carried directly by the winds that dance through the forest canopy.Visiting the Subramanya Temple at Pambar Shola isn’t just a temple visit—it’s a journey inward, a sacred pause in a wild world, and a reminder that true divinity often hides where the forest breathes.

Opening Hours

  • 7:00 AM – 11:00 AM
  • 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM

Festive Seasons

  • Thaipusam (January–February – major event with processions and Kavadi).
  • Skanda Sashti (October–November – six-day festival celebrating Lord Muruga’s victory over evil).

Entry Fees:

  • No Entry Fee – Special poojas may have additional charges

Transportation Options

  • From Kodaikanal Bus stand - 6 km
  • Mode of Transport: Auto or taxi
  • Fare: ₹150-₹200 for auto, ₹250-₹350 for taxi

Nearby Heritage Stays & Hotels

  • The Tamara Kodai (1.4 km) - 5-star heritage resort offering luxury in a colonial setting with fine dining.
  • Great Trails Kodaikanal by GRT (1.2 km) – Premium stay surrounded by nature with luxury cottages and bonfire spaces.
  • Lilly’s Valley Resort (1.6 km) – Eco-resort built with natural materials, loved by eco-conscious travelers.

Famous Food Places

  • Tava Veg Restaurant (1.4 km) - Pocket-friendly spot for Indian thalis and chapati-based meals.
  • Potluck Café (1.5 km) - Laid-back vibe with fusion dishes, healthy bowls, and smoothies.
  • Eco Nut Café (1.6 km) - Organic café serving vegan-friendly and gluten-free food options.
