Green Valley View / Suicide Point (Kodaikanal)

The Ultimate Canvas – Where Nature Paints with Light and Shadow!

Watch the sun weave magic across valleys, mountains, and rivers.

Viewpoint Highlights

Previously known as Suicide Point, Green Valley View is a breathtaking viewpoint that offers panoramic views of the deep valleys, lush forests, and distant plains below. Perched at a high altitude, this viewpoint gets its former name due to the steep drop of over 5,000 feet into the valley. The best time to visit is during the morning when the skies are clear, allowing visitors to witness the vast expanse of greenery stretching endlessly. The viewpoint is also famous for its bustling local market, where tourists can buy souvenirs, handmade chocolates, and traditional woolen products. Despite the eerie name, Green Valley View remains one of the most scenic spots in Kodaikanal, providing an unforgettable experience for visitors.

Opening Hours

  • 7:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Best Time to Visit

  • Kodaikanal Summer Festival (May): A fantastic time to visit, where nature is at its peak beauty.
