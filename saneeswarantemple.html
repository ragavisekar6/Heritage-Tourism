Saneeswaran Temple

Myth, Majesty & Miracles – A Temple’s Timeless Embrace!

Every stone whispers legends of gods, saints, and devotees who shaped the soul of this divine space.

Temple Highlights

Saneeswaran Temple in Kuchanur, Theni district, is one of the few temples in India dedicated solely to Lord Shani (Saneeswaran). It is believed that the deity here resides in the form of “Swayambu” (self-manifested) without a statue. Devotees from all over Tamil Nadu and Kerala visit this temple to seek blessings and relief from Shani dosha. The temple is located on the banks of the Surabi river, adding to its divine and peaceful atmosphere.

Opening Hours

6:00 AM – 12:00 PM, 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Festive Seasons

  • Sani Peyarchi Festival (every 2.5 years) – Grand celebrations and special rituals during Saturn transit
  • Amavasya (New Moon Days) – Special pujas and large gatherings of devotees
