Mannavanur Lake (Kodaikanal)

Boat Rides, Breezy Skies, and a Heart Full of Peace!

Glide across the shimmering waters and embrace the rhythm of nature.

Lake Highlights

Mannavanur Lake is a hidden paradise, located around 35 km from Kodaikanal, offering an idyllic escape from the tourist crowds. This pristine lake is surrounded by lush meadows, rolling hills, and dense pine forests, creating a picturesque landscape that looks straight out of a fairy tale. The drive to Mannavanur is an experience in itself, with scenic vistas, charming villages, and occasional encounters with grazing sheep and wild animals. The lake is part of an eco-tourism initiative, and visitors can indulge in activities like coracle rides, birdwatching, and nature walks. The area is also home to the Mannavanur Sheep Farm, operated by the Central Sheep and Wool Research Institute, where visitors can observe different breeds of sheep and rabbits. The tranquil atmosphere, combined with the crisp mountain air, makes it an ideal spot for meditation, photography, and relaxation. Picnicking by the lake while soaking in the panoramic views of the Western Ghats is an experience not to be missed. Mannavanur Lake is a perfect destination for those seeking peace, natural beauty, and a deeper connection with nature.

Monsoon Season (June–September): The lake looks stunning surrounded by mist and greenery.
