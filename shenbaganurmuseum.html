Shenbaganur Museum (Kodaikanal)

A Treasure Trove of History, A Journey Through Time!

From ancient relics to priceless manuscripts, these museums preserve the whispers of the past. Every exhibit is a time capsule, waiting to unveil secrets of bygone eras.

Museum Highlights

For history and nature enthusiasts, the Shenbaganur Museum of Natural History is a treasure trove of information about the region’s biodiversity and cultural heritage. Maintained by the Sacred Heart College, the museum houses an impressive collection of rare orchids, preserved animals, and ancient artifacts related to the indigenous tribes of Kodaikanal. One of the key attractions here is the extensive collection of over 300 species of orchids, making it a haven for botany lovers. The museum also features mounted specimens of local wildlife, including birds and mammals native to the region. Established in 1895, this museum offers a fascinating insight into the ecological and anthropological history of Kodaikanal. If you are interested in learning more about the flora, fauna, and history of the hill station, Shenbaganur Museum is a must-visit destination.
