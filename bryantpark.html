Bryant Park (Kodaikanal)

A Symphony of Colors – Where Flowers Bloom and Birds Sing!

Lose yourself in the vibrant beauty of meticulously crafted gardens.

Park Highlights

Bryant Park is a beautifully landscaped botanical garden located near Kodaikanal Lake. Spanning across 20 acres, this lush green park is home to over 325 species of trees, shrubs, and cacti, making it a paradise for plant lovers and botany enthusiasts. It was established in 1908 by British officer H.D. Bryant, after whom the park is named. The highlight of Bryant Park is its vibrant rose garden, which boasts an impressive variety of roses in full bloom. The park also features a greenhouse with exotic plant species and an ornamental garden that attracts thousands of tourists each year. The best time to visit is during the annual flower show held in May, where visitors can witness a spectacular display of colorful flowers and rare plant species. Whether you want to enjoy a leisurely stroll, have a family picnic, or simply admire nature’s beauty, Bryant Park is an ideal place to unwind in Kodaikanal.

Opening Hours

9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Festival:

Flower Show (May): The Bryant Park Flower Show is a must-visit for flower lovers and photographers.
