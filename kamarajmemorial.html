Kamaraj Memorial House, Teynampet

A Symbol of Struggle, Strength, and Sacrifice!

Pay tribute to the souls who shaped the destiny of generations.

Memorial Highlights

The Kamaraj Memorial House, located in T. Nagar, is dedicated to the memory of K. Kamaraj, former Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu and freedom fighter. The house has been preserved with his personal belongings, photographs, and historical artifacts, providing a glimpse into his simple lifestyle and monumental contributions to the education sector.

Opening Hours

10:00 AM – 5:00 PM; closed on Mondays.

Transportation Options

  • Located in Teynampet; accessible via buses and autos.

Entry Fees:

  • Free.
