Sacred Heart Church

Where Faith Rests in Every Brick and Hope Soars in Every Prayer!

Step inside these magnificent churches and let the peaceful silence speak to your soul. With towering spires, colorful stained glass, and centuries-old hymns, they are sanctuaries of grace and devotion.

Church Highlights

Sacred Heart Church in Theni is a prominent Roman Catholic church known for its peaceful atmosphere and vibrant community. The church hosts regular Sunday masses and grand Christmas celebrations, drawing large gatherings.

Opening Hours

  • 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Festive Seasons

  • Christmas (December 25th) – Midnight mass & celebrations
  • Feast of Sacred Heart (June) – Special prayers & feasts
