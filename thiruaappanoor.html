Thiru Aappanoor Temple

Where the Divine Resides, and Legends Come Alive!

In these hallowed shrines, history and mythology merge into a mystical experience. Feel the divine energy, admire the intricate sculptures, and immerse yourself in an atmosphere where devotion echoes through the ages.

Temple Highlights

Nestled in the heart of Sellur, Madurai, Thiru Aappanoor Temple is an ancient and revered Shiva temple with deep spiritual significance. Dedicated to Lord Shiva in the form of Aappudaiyaar, the temple is steeped in rich mythology and is believed to be a site where Lord Shiva granted divine blessings to his devotees. The presiding deity, Aappudaiyaar, is worshipped alongside Goddess Thirupurasundari, symbolizing the divine union of cosmic energy and grace.Architecturally, the temple reflects the grandeur of Dravidian craftsmanship, with intricately carved pillars, ornate sculptures, and a majestic gopuram. Every corner of the temple resonates with spiritual energy, drawing devotees seeking peace, prosperity, and liberation from worldly struggles. The temple is particularly vibrant during Maha Shivaratri, when thousands of devotees gather for night-long prayers, and Pradosham, a bi-monthly occasion for special rituals.The temple’s serene ambiance, coupled with the rhythmic chanting of hymns and the scent of incense, makes it a perfect place for meditation and spiritual rejuvenation. With its deep-rooted traditions, captivating legends, and awe-inspiring architecture, Thiru Aappanoor Temple stands as a beacon of devotion, offering a timeless connection between humanity and the divine.

Opening Hours

  • Morning: 5:30 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Evening: 4:00 PM - 8:30 PM

Festive Seasons

  • Pradosham – Observed twice a month with elaborate rituals.
  • Karthigai Deepam – Thousands of lamps light up the temple in November-December.
  • Aani Thirumanjanam – Special abhishekam to Lord Shiva in June-July.
  • Navaratri – Dedicated poojas for Goddess Thirupurasundari.
  • Maha Shivaratri – Grand night-long celebrations with special abhishekams.

Entry Fees: No Entry Fee – Special poojas may have additional charges
