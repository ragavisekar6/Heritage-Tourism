Kodaikanal Lake

A Liquid Jewel in the Heart of Nature!

Let the serenity of crystal-clear waters wash away your worries.

Lake Highlights

Kodaikanal Lake is the heart of this charming hill station, offering breathtaking views and a serene ambiance. This star-shaped artificial lake, created in 1863 by Sir Vere Henry Levinge, is spread over 60 acres and is surrounded by lush greenery and mist-covered hills. Visitors can indulge in a variety of recreational activities such as boating, cycling, and horse riding along the lake's scenic pathway. Pedal boats, rowboats, and shikaras are available for rent, making it a delightful experience for families and couples alike. The lake is particularly mesmerizing during sunrise and sunset when the golden hues reflect off the tranquil waters. Tourists can also visit the nearby shopping stalls selling handmade chocolates, woolen clothes, and handicrafts. The annual boat pageant and flower show conducted here add to its charm. Whether you want to take a peaceful stroll along the shore, enjoy the cool breeze, or capture postcard-perfect photographs, Kodaikanal Lake is a must-visit attraction that embodies the beauty of this hill station.
