Dolphin's Nose (Kodaikanal)

A Window to the World – Where Earth Unfolds Beneath Your Feet!

Stand at the edge of wonder and let the breathtaking views steal your heart.

Viewpoint Highlights

Dolphin’s Nose is a popular trekking destination in Kodaikanal, known for its unique rock formation that resembles a dolphin’s nose. The viewpoint offers a breathtaking view of the lush valleys and rolling hills below. Adventure seekers can trek through the forest trails to reach this spot, enjoying the fresh mountain air and stunning landscapes along the way. The best time to visit is during the early morning hours when the mist lifts, revealing the scenic beauty of the region. The sheer drop at the edge of the rock makes it an exhilarating yet thrilling experience for visitors.

Opening Hours

  • 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Best Time to Visit

  • Summer Festival (May): Enjoy panoramic views and breathtaking landscapes during this time.

Entry Fees:

  • No such Entry fee.

Transportation Options

  • From Kodaikanal Bus Stand: 12 km.
  • Mode of Transport: Taxi, or Auto.
  • Fare: ₹300-₹400 for taxi.

Nearby Heritage Stays & Hotels

  • The Tamara Kodaikanal - Luxury resort with panoramic views, ~10 km from Dolphin’s Nose.
  • Kodai Resort Hotel - A heritage hotel near the lake and Dolphin’s Nose.
