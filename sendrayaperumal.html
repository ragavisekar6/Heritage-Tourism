Sendraya Perumal Temple (Batlagundu, Dindigul)

A Divine Canvas Painted with Faith and Time!

Every sculpture, every deity, every motif is a masterpiece of devotion.

Temple Highlights

Perched on a picturesque hill in Batlagundu, Sendraya Perumal Kovil is a unique temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu. The temple’s location amidst the lush greenery and rocky terrain makes it a breathtaking sight, attracting both devotees and nature lovers.Sendraya Perumal, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu, is worshipped here as the guardian deity of the region. The temple has a powerful aura, and it is believed that sincere prayers here can remove obstacles and bring prosperity. Devotees often climb the hill barefoot as an act of devotion, seeking blessings for health and success.One of the special features of this temple is its connection to Ramayana legends. Many believe that Lord Rama himself prayed here before embarking on his journey to Lanka. The temple also holds annual festivals, with Vaikunta Ekadasi and Purattasi Saturdays being the most significant.For those who love adventure, the climb to the temple offers a thrilling experience, with scenic views of the surrounding landscape. The temple's peaceful ambiance, coupled with its divine presence, makes it an ideal place for meditation and prayer.

Opening Hours

  • 6:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Festive Seasons

  • Vaikunta Ekadashi: Typically observed in December-January, marking an auspicious day dedicated to Lord Vishnu.
  • Purattasi Saturdays: Falling between September and October, these Saturdays are considered sacred for Lord Vishnu devotees.

Entry Fees:
