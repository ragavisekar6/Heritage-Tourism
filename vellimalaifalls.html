Vellimalai Falls (Sirumalai)

A Symphony of Falling Waters, A Canvas of Pure Bliss!

Marvel at the breathtaking beauty of nature’s masterpiece as crystal-clear waters tumble from towering heights, creating an oasis of tranquility and wonder.

Falls Highlights

Vellimalai Falls, located in the picturesque Vellimalai range of the Western Ghats, is a hidden gem in the Dindigul district of Tamil Nadu. The waterfall is nestled amidst lush green forests and offers a serene and tranquil atmosphere, making it an ideal destination for nature enthusiasts, trekkers, and those looking for an escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. The falls cascade down from a height of approximately 300 feet, creating a mesmerizing sight, especially during the monsoon season when the flow is at its peak.The surrounding area is rich in biodiversity, with dense vegetation and a variety of wildlife, including birds and butterflies, making it an excellent spot for wildlife photography and birdwatching. The trek to the falls offers a unique experience, with winding paths through the forest and scenic views of the surrounding hills and valleys.Visitors to Vellimalai Falls can enjoy a peaceful day surrounded by nature, with opportunities for hiking, photography, and picnics. The falls are located in a relatively secluded area, adding to their charm. The best time to visit is during the monsoon or post-monsoon season, when the falls are in full flow and the landscape is at its greenest.

Opening Hours

6:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Best Time to Visit

September to February, during the monsoon and post-monsoon seasons, when the waterfall is in full flow.

Entry Fees: No such Entry fee.

Transportation Options

  • From Dindigul Bus Stand (28 km): Private vehicle preferred; no regular bus. Hire jeep/auto from Sirumalai road junction. Taxi fare: ₹900–₹1,100.
  • Dindigul Junction (27 km) – Taxi fare: ₹900–₹1,000.
  • Madurai Airport (99 km) – Taxi fare: ₹1,900–₹2,300.

Nearby Heritage Stays & Hotels

  • Vellimalai Resort (located near the falls, approx. 10-15 km away).

Famous Food Places

  • Vasantha Hotel (approx. 20 km) – A local favorite for authentic South Indian meals.
  • Saravana Bhavan (approx. 18 km) – Known for its vegetarian delicacies.

Must-Try Specialties

  • Sirumalai Banana (Malai Vazhai Pazham) – Local farms & roadside vendors in Sirumalai.
  • Jackfruit Dishes (Palapazham Delicacies) – Sirumalai Jackfruit Farms, Local Homestays.
  • Herbal Honey – Sirumalai Honey Farms, Street-side Vendors.
  • Forest Mushroom Biryani – Small eateries & homestays in Sirumalai.
  • Tamarind-Based Dishes (Puli Kuzhambu & Rasam) – Homestay Kitchens, Local Messes.
  • Fresh Herbal Teas & Kashayam – Local Tea Stalls & Ayurvedic Stores.
  • Spicy Banana Leaf Fish Fry – Homestay Kitchens & Local Street Vendors.
  • Sirumalai Special Fresh Fruits & Pickles – Local Farms & Roadside Fruit Vendors.
