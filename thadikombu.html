Soundararaja Perumal Temple (Thadikombu, Dindigul)

Step Through the Gateway of the Gods, Into an Eternal Embrace!

Be part of a legacy that has been preserved in devotion for centuries.

Temple Highlights

Thadikombu Perumal Kovil, located near Dindigul, is an ancient temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu in his Perumal form. This temple, believed to be over 500 years old, is a masterpiece of Dravidian architecture, with a towering rajagopuram (entrance tower) and intricately carved pillars showcasing mythological tales.One of the most fascinating aspects of this temple is the belief that praying to Lord Perumal here removes sins and grants peace and prosperity. The temple is famous for its annual Chitra Pournami festival, during which devotees gather in large numbers to witness the grand procession of the deity and special rituals.The temple is surrounded by lush greenery, making it a serene and spiritually uplifting place. Devotees often perform "pradakshina" (circumambulation) around the sanctum as a form of prayer. The temple’s sacred tank is another highlight, where devotees take holy dips before offering prayers. With its rich history, divine energy, and stunning architecture, Thadikombu Perumal Kovil is a must-visit for Vishnu devotees and heritage enthusiasts.

Festive Seasons

  • Brahmotsavam: An annual festival featuring processions and special rituals, attracting numerous devotees.
  • Vaikunta Ekadasi, a significant festival dedicated to Lord Vishnu, is typically observed in December-January in Vishnu temples across Tamil Nadu.
