Kolli Hills Forest Reserve (Trichy Region Access)

The Untamed Lungs of Tamil Nadu’s Eastern Ghats!

Beyond the winding ghats and misty slopes lies a forest whispering secrets of sages, herbs, and ancient silence.

Reserve Highlights

This dense forest reserve in the Kolli Hills is known for its biodiversity and sacred legends. It houses medicinal plants, exotic bird species, and even tribal settlements. The area is believed to be blessed by Kollipavai, a guardian goddess. It is a paradise for trekkers, botanists, and nature lovers. Numerous trails, view points, and waterfalls crisscross the forested landscape.
